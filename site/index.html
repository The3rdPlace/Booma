This is Booma - a SDR VLF receiver born out of curiosity while listening to the SAQ / Grimeton transmitter using a WebSDR receiver somewhere in Germany.

Currently, all Booma can do, is to listen to frequencies in the audio range (using a soundcard as the receiver frontend), and detecting CW and RTTY signals. However, I have big plans for extending the basic receiver for use with RTL based sdr dongles and, at least, listning on the LW, MW and Shortwave bands.. But for now, its a (not very fancy) receiver for the SAQ transmitter.

You can download a deb package for linux (debian 9 or there about) here: booma booma_{MAJORVERSION}.{MINORVERSION}-{BUILD}_amd64.deb or you can build from the sources found at Github: https://github.com/The3rdPlace/Booma.git

Booma requires the Hardt toolkit (The Hamradio DSP Toolkit) found here http://hardttoolkit.org/ and PortAudio http://www.portaudio.com/
